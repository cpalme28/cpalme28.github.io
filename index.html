Family History

The Palmers, as are known today, begin with John and Marietta Palmer, the patriarch and matriarch of the family. They met and their love grew when they first met each other while working for the airlines. Once married, they moved to Arizona and started a family of their own. Their children are Patty Odle, Brenda Berch, and Clint Palmer. The three of them married their partners and started families of their own. John and Marietta now have eight grandchildren by birth (10 through marriage) and two great-grandchildren (almost three).
